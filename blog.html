Important FAQ

Why is it important to donate for Noakhali and Feni?

The recent floods in Noakhali and Feni have caused significant damage. Donations are crucial to provide essential supplies like food, water, and medicine, and to help rebuild the affected communities.

How can I make a donation?

You can donate by entering the amount in the donation input field under the specific project and clicking the "Donate Now" button. Your donation will go directly to support the relief efforts.

How secure is the donation process?

Our donation platform is completely secure and reliable. Every donation is carefully tracked, and funds are distributed to the respective relief projects efficiently.

How much should I donate?

You can donate any amount according to your capacity. Every contribution, big or small, is valuable and makes a difference to those in need.

How can I view my donation history?

You can view your donation history by clicking on the "History" tab. It will display all your past donations, including the date, time, and amount.
